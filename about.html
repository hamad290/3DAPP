About Us

Coca-Cola is a carbonated soft drink manufactured by The Coca-Cola Company, headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. It was originally intended as a patent medicine when it was invented in the late 19th century by pharmacist John Stith Pemberton.

Coca-Cola was introduced in 1886, and over the years, it has become one of the most recognized and popular brands in the world. It is sold in more than 200 countries and is available in various flavors and versions.

The Coca-Cola Company has a long history of marketing campaigns, sponsorships, and partnerships, making Coca-Cola one of the most iconic brands globally.
